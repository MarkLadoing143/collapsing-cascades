Oliver Leung Wei-Shan / 梁偉善

Originally from Hong Kong, Oliver is a solemn man who recently graduated at Simon Fraser University, and now works as an engineer in Downtown Vancover. Generally laid-back and indifferent to most situations, Oliver is a mostly quiet individual who feels more comfortable on his own then with others, in turn causing him to be more distant and awkward in social situations. But despite his cold exterior, Oliver truly cares for those around him and while he may not always show his emotions, deep down he is warm-hearted and humble.

Born in a former industrial area near the now-closed Kai Tak International Airport, his father (a Royal Hong Kong Police Officer) and his mother (an Secondary School Teacher) decided to leave Hong Kong in 1995 fearing the impending Handover to China, eventually immigrating to Canada and settling in the city of Victoria, British Columbia. Despite not leaving Hong Kong at the age of three, Oliver is bilingual in both Cantonese and English, and has frequently associated with Chinese Canadian Organizations due to his parents' encouragement. In line with his roots, Oliver is a fan of English Football, following teams both in Hong Kong and Canada. Now living Port Coquitlam on the mainland, he now routinely commutes to Vancouver for work, and has a strong personal friendship with fellow co-worker Joel Dacquel.
